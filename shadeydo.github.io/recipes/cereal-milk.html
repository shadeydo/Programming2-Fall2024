Cereal and Milk

Description

It's cereal mixed with milk.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cereal
  • 1 cup milk
  • Spoon
  • Bowl
  • At least one hand with an opposable thumb attached to a funmctional arm

Steps:

  1. Put the bowl on the table.
  2. Grab your cereal of choice and pour into the bowl.
  3. Put milk in bowl.
  4. Put spoon in bowl and enjoy!
