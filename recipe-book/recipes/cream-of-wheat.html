Description

Like pig slop but for humans! Creamy gelatenous homogenized wheat goop mixed with brown sugar and butter.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbs Cream of Wheat
  • 1-1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbs brown sugar
  • Optional: 1 tbs butter

Steps:

  1. Bring milk to a boil in a small sized pot.
  2. Pour in Cream of Wheat while whisking milk so as to avoid clumping.
  3. Turn heat to low and simmer while stirring occasionally for 2-3 minutes, or until mixture is at your preferred viscosity.
  4. Mix in brown sugar and/or butter for a richer flavor.
