Hot Pretzels

Description

Warm fluffy yeast pockets with a ton of salt on top.

Ingredients:

  • 1/8 cup warm water
  • 1 Package active dry yeast
  • 1-1/3 cups water
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • ~6 cups flour
  • Coarse kosher salt
  • 1/3 cup baking soda

Steps:

  1. Combine yeast, warm water, and a pinch of brown sugar in a glass.
  2. Let mixture rise in a warm location for 30 minutes.
  3. Pour into a large bowl and mix in the rest of the brown sugar.
  4. Slowly add flour while mixing until mixture becomes too thick to stir.
  5. Dump mixture on a lightly floured surface and knead in remaining flour until dough becomes thick enough to form.
  6. Divide dough into 6-8 palm sized lumps, forming them into desired shapes.
  7. In a large shallow pot filled with water, add Baking soda and bring to a boil.
  8. Place a pretzel in the pot and leave it floating in the water for 30 seconds, then take it out to dry on a baking sheet.
  9. Repeat with all pretzels.
  10. Heavily salt pretzels then cook in a 475 degree oven for 10 minutes.
